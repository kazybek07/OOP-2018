About Us

OnClinic is focused on being an independent Critical Access Hospital and Rural Health Clinic that improves the health of those we serve. Through prudent management of its staff and facilities and relationships with regional providers, OnClinic provides a wide range of medical services while operating in a cost effective manner. OnClinic staff takes individual and collective responsibility for its actions and works as a cooperative, integrated team that uses its skills for the benefit of the patient and organization.

Through the years we have helped generation after generation reach better health. Our organization has been striving to serve the needs of our region since we were established. We here at OnClinic are loyal to our community and will continue to strive for excellence in healthcare.
