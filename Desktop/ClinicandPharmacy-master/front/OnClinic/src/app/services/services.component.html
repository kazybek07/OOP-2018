Services

  • Acute Care

    OnClinic is a Critical Access Hospital providing primary, preventive and emergency health care services. The acute care component of OnClinic's services is comprised of 10 designated swing beds (may be used for either acute or long term care). Also available is a 24-hour emergency room services and full service lab and x-ray departments.

  • Laboratory and X-Ray

    OnClinic has both laboratory and X-Ray capabilities on site. The lab is available for both in-patient and out-patient services. It is also convenient to the patients of the OnClinic Family Medicine Clinic.
